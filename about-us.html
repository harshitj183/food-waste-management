Welcome to Food Link

About Us

Welcome to Food Link, where we are dedicated to making a positive impact by reducing food waste and addressing hunger in our community. Our mission is to connect food donors, NGOs, and those in need to create a sustainable and efficient food distribution network.

Our Mission

At Food Link, we believe in the power of collaboration to tackle the challenges of food waste and hunger. Our mission is to:

  • Reduce food waste by connecting food donors with surplus food to NGOs in need.
  • Address hunger by ensuring that edible food reaches those who need it the most.
  • Build a community that cares about sustainability and social responsibility.

How It Works

Our platform facilitates the seamless donation of surplus food from restaurants, hotels, and individuals. The process involves:

  1. Donors creating an account and listing available food items on our platform.
  2. NGOs accessing the platform, reviewing available donations, and arranging for pickups.
  3. Delivery personnel ensuring that the donated food reaches its destination safely and promptly.
